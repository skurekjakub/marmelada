    Activity type

    Activity type name (code name)

    Logged when

    Blog comment

    blogcomment

    Visitors comment on a blog post.

    Blog post subscription

    blogpostsubscription

    Contacts subscribe to blog post comments.

    Clicked link in marketing email

    newsletterclickthrough

    Contacts click a tracked link in a marketing email.

    Content rating

    rating

    Visitors rate content (see Content rating).

    Custom table form submit

    customtablesubmit

    Visitors submit a custom table form.

    Event booking

    eventbooking

    Contacts sign up for an event using the Booking system.

    External search

    externalsearch

    Visitors come to the website after searching on a search engine, such as Google.

    Form submission

    bizformsubmit

    Visitors submit an on-line form.

    Forum post

    forumpost

    Visitors post on a forum.

    Forum post subscription

    forumpostsubscription

    Contacts subscribe to a forum post.

    Internal search

    internalsearch

    Visitors search for text using the on-site search.

    Landing page

    landingpage

    Visitors open the page when first viewing the website. (The cookie key affecting the activity logging expires after 20 minutes and the expiration period is renewed every time the website is accessed again.)

    Message board comment

    messageboardcomment

    Visitors post a comment on a message board.

    Message board subscription

    messageboardsubscription

    Contacts subscribe to a message board.

    Opened marketing email

    newsletteropen

    Contacts open a tracked marketing email.

    Opted out from all marketing emails

    newsletterunsubscriptionfromall

    Contacts opt out from all marketing emails.

    Page visit

    pagevisit

    Visitors view a page.

    Poll voting

    pollvoting

    Visitors vote in a poll.

    Product added to shopping cart

    productaddedtoshoppingcart

    Visitors add a product to the shopping cart.

    Product added to wishlist

    productaddedtowishlist

    Visitors add a product to their wishlist.

    Product removed from shopping cart

    productremovedfromshoppingcart

    Visitors remove a product from the shopping cart.

    Purchase

    purchase

    Customers make a purchase.

    Purchased product

    purchasedproduct

    Customers buy a specific product.

    Shopping cart abandoned

    ShoppingCartAbandoned

    Visitors put products into the shopping cart and then leave the site. After a specified amount of time, the shopping cart is considered as abandoned.

    Subscription to a newsletter

    newslettersubscription

    Contacts subscribe to a newsletter.

    Unsubscription from a single email feed

    newsletterunsubscription

    Contacts unsubscribe from a single email feed.

    User login

    userlogin

    Users log in to a site.

    User registration

    userregistration

    Contacts register as a member of a site.
