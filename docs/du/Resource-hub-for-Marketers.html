    Tutorials and courses

    Are you starting with managing content or running campaigns on a new Kentico website? Kentico training courses for marketers and editors and other resources like our documentation or tutorial will help you get quickly on board. And if you are not sure what is the typical learning path for new Kentico users, you can check out the flow of our training courses and other training materials.

    Kentico MVC Business Tutorial

    A hands-on, step-by-step guide helps editors with no previous knowledge of Kentico. You’ll learn how to edit pages or work with page attachments on Kentico MVC websites while working with a hosted Dancing Goat MVC sample site.

    Kentico MVC for Marketers and Editors

    The e-learning course teaches content administrators and marketers who are new to Kentico and digital marketing. You’ll learn how to maintain content on the website, set up campaigns, run your email marketing activities, and handle most other common digital marketing tasks.

    Content Admin Essentials

    The e-learning course aims at content administrators new to Kentico who work with websites built on the Portal Engine technology. You will learn how to add, structure, and maintain content on your site and perform the most common web editor tasks.

    Online Marketing Essentials

    The e-learning course will help you learn about the core capabilities and master the essential skills for working with Kentico EMS on websites built on the Portal Engine technology. You will learn how to set up campaigns, run your email marketing activities, and handle most other common digital marketing tasks.

    What if I need help?

    After completing our business courses, you should be ready to work with the content of your website and carry out marketing activities with Kentico on your own.

    If you need a quick check on how to work with Kentico features, the Documentation is just a mouse-click away. You can always contact our Support team. And if you run into any difficulties with your website implementation, our experienced marketing consultants are here to help you.

    Marketing Certification

    The certification verifies your skill in working with Kentico, your knowledge of digital marketing practices, and helps you become an internationally recognized Kentico Certified Marketer. The online exam tests advanced knowledge and skills of running marketing activities in Kentico – further Kentico experience beyond the training course content is necessary.
